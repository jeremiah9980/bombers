POLICY DEVELOPMENT

All organizational policies are developed through a collaborative process involving board input, coaching staff feedback, and parent perspectives where appropriate.

Policies are reviewed annually or as needed when circumstances change. Significant policy changes require board approval according to our bylaws voting structure.

For questions about any policy or to request a copy of a specific document, please contact the board through our contact page.
