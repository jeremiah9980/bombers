RALLYIQ

Our AI-powered team operating system for modern sports organization management.

WHAT IS RALLYIQ?

RallyIQ is an AI-powered team operating system designed specifically for youth sports organizations. It combines team management, communication, fundraising, and analytics into a single platform that helps coaches, administrators, and families stay connected and organized.

CORE MODULES

Integrated tools for complete team management

🏆

COACH MODULE

Practice planning, drill libraries, lineup builders, and game preparation tools. Helps coaches organize sessions and track player progress throughout the season.

👥

TEAMS MODULE

Roster management, schedule coordination, availability tracking, and team communications. One place for all team information and announcements.

👤

PROFILES MODULE

Player profiles with stats, development tracking, and achievement records. Parents can view their player's progress and key milestones.

💰

FUNDRAISE MODULE

Digital fundraising campaigns, donation tracking, sponsor management, and financial reporting. Simplifies team fundraising efforts.

🔍

SCOUT MODULE

Opponent research, game video analysis, and scouting reports. Helps coaching staff prepare for upcoming competition.

🏢

ORG MODULE

Organization-wide management for multi-team programs. Budget tracking, board communications, and policy management.

AI ASSISTANCE MODES

Intelligent help when you need it

💬

ASSISTANT MODE

Natural language queries for quick answers. Ask about schedules, policies, player info, or anything else and get instant responses.

📝

DRAFT MODE

AI-powered content creation for announcements, emails, social posts, and documents. Get professional communications in seconds.

📊

ANALYZE MODE

Data insights and trend analysis across your organization. Understand patterns in performance, attendance, and engagement.

💡

SUGGEST MODE

Proactive recommendations for practices, lineups, and organizational decisions based on historical data and best practices.
