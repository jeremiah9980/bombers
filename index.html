Team Info

Bombers Team Information

CTX Bombers Meza is a 10U select fastpitch softball team based in Georgetown, Texas, built for athletes who want to train with purpose, compete with confidence, and prepare for the next level of the game.

Our mission is to develop elite athletes through disciplined preparation, high standards, game IQ, toughness, and a team-first culture that pushes every player to grow on and off the field.

We are proud to represent Bomber Nation, a championship softball family creating champions since 2001. #BlueBloods

The roster lives on its own page at /roster/, with player cards, profile links, and image paths from assets/players/firstname-lastinitial.jpg.
