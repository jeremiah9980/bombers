Transparency

FINANCES

Complete transparency in how we fund and operate our program. Every dollar accounted for.

CORE COMMITMENT TO TRANSPARENCY

We believe families deserve to know exactly where their money goes. Our financial model is built on transparency, accountability, and responsible stewardship. Every expense is documented, every decision is recorded, and financial reports are available to all team families.

REVENUE SOURCES

Our organization is funded through multiple sources to ensure sustainability and keep individual family costs manageable.

TEAM FEES

Seasonal fees covering core operations, coaching, and baseline tournament participation.

TOURNAMENT FEES

Per-tournament costs collected as needed based on team schedule.

FUNDRAISING

Team fundraising events that reduce family out-of-pocket costs.

SPONSORSHIPS

Business partnerships providing equipment, uniforms, or financial support.

DONATIONS

Individual and family contributions supporting program growth.

GRANTS

Community grants for youth athletics and player assistance programs.

BUDGET ALLOCATION MODEL

How we allocate resources - The 40/30/20/10 Model

40%

TOURNAMENTS

Entry fees, umpire costs, and travel-related tournament expenses.

30%

EQUIPMENT

Team equipment, practice supplies, uniforms, and gear maintenance.

20%

OPERATIONS

Field rentals, insurance, league fees, and administrative costs.

10%

RESERVE

Emergency fund and player assistance program support.

FREE PLAY POLICY

No player will be turned away due to financial hardship. Our Player Assistance Program, funded by the reserve allocation and dedicated donations, ensures every talented athlete can participate regardless of economic circumstances. This commitment is at the core of who we are.

TOURNAMENT COSTS

Tournament expenses vary by event tier and location. Below are typical cost ranges for different tournament types.

Local One-Day Tournament $200-$350
Regional Weekend Tournament $350-$550
Multi-Day Travel Tournament $500-$800
Championship/National Event $800-$1,200+

Note: These are entry fee ranges only. Additional travel, lodging, and meal costs are typically family responsibility.

FINANCIAL OVERSIGHT

Multiple layers of oversight ensure financial integrity and responsible stewardship of organizational funds.

DUAL SIGNATURE

All expenditures above $500 require approval from two Co-Directors before payment.

QUARTERLY REPORTS

Financial reports shared with all team families each quarter showing income, expenses, and balances.

RECEIPT DOCUMENTATION

Every expense documented with receipts and categorized for transparent record-keeping.

ANNUAL REVIEW

Full financial review at end of each season with comparison to budget projections.
