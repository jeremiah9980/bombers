Leadership

BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Professional governance ensuring accountability, transparency, and sustainable excellence.

EXECUTIVE LEADERSHIP

Three Co-Directors providing balanced oversight and strategic direction

1

CO-DIRECTOR

Finance & Operations

Oversees budget management, financial reporting, vendor relationships, and operational logistics.

2

CO-DIRECTOR

Strategy & Development

Leads organizational planning, program development, partnerships, and long-term growth initiatives.

3

CO-DIRECTOR

Communications & Community

Manages parent communications, community relations, fundraising coordination, and brand presence.

VOTING BOARD MEMBERS

Diverse perspectives ensuring balanced decision-making

HEAD COACH REPRESENTATIVE

Brings coaching staff perspective to board decisions. Ensures player development remains central to all organizational choices.

PARENT REPRESENTATIVE

Elected by team families to voice parent concerns, feedback, and priorities. Serves as liaison between families and leadership.

OPEN POSITION

Reserved for future expansion. May include additional parent rep, community advisor, or specialist as organization grows.

DECISION-MAKING STRUCTURE

Three-tiered voting system for appropriate oversight

ORDINARY DECISIONS

Simple Majority Required

Day-to-day operational decisions that keep the organization running smoothly.

  • Practice schedule adjustments
  • Tournament registration
  • Equipment purchases under $500
  • Communication timing and methods

SIGNIFICANT DECISIONS

2/3 Majority Required

Important decisions that affect the organization's direction or finances.

  • Budget allocations over $500
  • Policy changes
  • Staff additions or changes
  • Partnership agreements

FOUNDATIONAL DECISIONS

Unanimous Consent Required

Core decisions that define the organization's identity and structure.

  • Bylaws amendments
  • Leadership changes
  • Organizational structure modifications
  • Mission or values changes

MEETING SCHEDULE

The Board meets quarterly to review operations, finances, and strategic direction. Additional meetings may be called for urgent matters requiring board action.
